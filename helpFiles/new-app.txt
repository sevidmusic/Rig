
 `ddms --new-app --name [--domain]`

  Description:

  Creates a new Darling Data Managment System App in the current working directory.

  Arguments:

  --name The name to assign to the new App

  [--domain] The default domain to assign to the new App. Default: http://localhost:8080/

  Examples:

  `ddms --new-app --name "HelloWorld"`

  `ddms --new-app --name "HelloWorld" --domain "http://localhost:8765"`
